FAQ

Is other sports shoes allowed?

Strictly No, only non-marking shoes are allowed. You can also play with bare foot also.

Any Racquet🏸 available for guest player?

Yes, you can rent the Racquet🏸 for ₹30/hour.

Should players bring the cork?

Yes, players should bring the cork. If they don't have, they can buy from us.

Do member's can't play on weekends?

If there is no booking done by guest players, then members can hapily😀 on weekends also.

How often you conduct internal tournaments?

Usually we conduct once in two months. In future we are planning to conduct once in month.
